Nowadays 30% of the population suffers from poor, restless sleeping habits and sleep disorders. The long-term effects of sleep deprivation are real.
It may cause health issues, weakened immune system, memory issues, high blood pressure, depressed mood.  When you don't get enough rest your concentration and problem-solving skills decrease. Being drowsy during the day can increase the risk for accidents.
Noticeable signs of sleep deprivation: excessive sleepiness, frequent yawning, irritability, daytime fatigue.
